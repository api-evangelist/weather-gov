# Weather.gov (weather-gov)
Weather.gov is the official website of the National Weather Service, which is responsible for providing accurate and timely weather forecasts, warnings, and advisories to the public. The website offers a wide range of weather information, including current conditions, radar images, satellite imagery, and forecasts for cities and regions across the United States. In addition to weather forecasts, Weather.gov also provides information on severe weather events such as hurricanes, tornadoes, and winter storms, as well as updates on ocean and air quality conditions. The website is a valuable resource for anyone looking to stay informed about the weather and plan their activities accordingly.
